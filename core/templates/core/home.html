What is Squire?

Squire is the new webapp developed by the HTTPS-committee of ESRG Knights of the Kitchen Table. Its goals are to ease certain tasks to be performed by the board, and to provide members with more control over their personal data.

Current Features

  • You can view and edit your own membership information at all times! No need to wait for the board to reply to your messages, and verifying it becomes much easier.
  • Achievements are back! Show of your skills or weird interactions in Boardgames, Roleplaying Sessions, or LARPs, and be an active member to earn some general achievements.

Future Plans

Oh boy are there many future features that we did not have the time for to develop before the first release. Do you have a (good) idea that is not on this list? Notify the board!

  • A Committee Overview: Are you a new (or old!) member and interested in our committees? Find them in a single place and see what they (should) do!
  • Email Opt-outs: Don't want to receive certain emails, such as the monthly agenda? Then an easy to manage opt-out system is key.
  • Delegating Tasks: The board (and admins) are currently the only people who can manage most things on this application, while some things (such as managing achievements) could also be done by certain committees instead!
